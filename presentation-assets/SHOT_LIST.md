## Recording Tips

- **Browser:** Chrome at 1280x800 or 1440x900 for clean screenshots
- **GIF tool:** Use macOS built-in screen recording (Cmd+Shift+5 → Record Selected Portion), then convert to GIF with any free tool, or use a dedicated app like Kap, Gifox, or CleanShot X
- **Consistent data:** Before capturing, set up 2-3 brand applications (mix of pending/approved) and 2-3 creator applications (mix of statuses) so dashboards look populated
- **Hide bookmarks bar** and browser extensions for clean shots
- **Crop** to just the app content (no browser chrome) for a polished look
